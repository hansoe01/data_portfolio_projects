## ☕ Coffee Shop Sales Analysis (Power BI)

### 📌 Project Summary
Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze coffee shop sales performance for the first half of 2023. The project focused on uncovering revenue drivers, customer behavior, and time-based sales trends to support data-driven decision-making.

### 🎯 Objectives
•	Identify top-performing products and categories <br>
•	Analyze sales trends by time of day and day of week <br>
•	Evaluate customer purchasing behavior (average order value, transaction volume) <br>
•	Provide actionable business insights and recommendations <br>

### 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
•	Power BI – Data visualization and dashboard development <br>
•	Power Query – Data cleaning and transformation <br>
•	DAX – Measures and calculated columns <br>

### 📈 Key Features of Dashboard

•	KPI cards for Total Sales ($699K), Transactions (149K), Avg Order Value ($4.69) <br>
•	Sales by Category to identify revenue drivers <br>
•	Top 10 Products by total sales <br>
•	Hourly Revenue Trends to highlight peak business hours <br>
•	Daily Heatmap for customer activity patterns <br>

### 🔍 Key Insights
•	Coffee is the primary revenue-generating category, significantly outperforming others <br>
•	A small group of products contributes to a large portion of total sales <br>
•	Morning hours (8–10 AM) generate the highest revenue <br>
•	Lower average order value suggests opportunities for upselling and bundling <br>
•	Sales decline during afternoon hours, indicating off-peak opportunities <br>

### 💡 Business Recommendations
•	Introduce bundle offers (coffee + bakery items) to increase order value <br>
•	Optimize staffing allocation and inventory during peak morning hours for better customer service 
and supply chain optimization <br>
•	Promote top-selling products to maximize revenue <br>
•	Launch afternoon promotions to improve low-demand periods <br>
•	Re-evaluate underperforming product categories and replace with new product if necessary <br>
