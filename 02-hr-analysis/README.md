## 📊 HR People Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

### 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive HR analytics dashboard built in Power BI to analyze workforce composition and employee turnover trends.

The dashboard helps organizations make data-driven HR decisions by uncovering insights into employee demographics, job structure, and attrition drivers.

### 🎯 Objectives
•	Analyze overall headcount distribution across departments, job levels, and locations <br>
•	Track employee turnover trends over time <br>
•	Identify key drivers of attrition <br> 
•	Evaluate workforce demographics (gender, age, education, ethnicity) <br>
•	Provide actionable insights for HR strategy and workforce planning <br>

### 🔍 Key Insights

**Workforce Insights**

•	Majority of employees are individual contributors (75%+) <br>
•	Workforce is highly remote (80%) <br>
•	Employee base is concentrated in Software and Sales departments <br>
•	Talent is clustered in major cities like San Francisco and Los Angeles <br>

**Demographics**

•	Workforce is male-dominated (~61%) <br>
•	Most employees fall within the 30–45 age range <br>
•	Majority hold a Bachelor’s degree <br>

**Turnover Insights**

•	Peak turnover observed in 2014 (6.78%) and 2021 (5.10%) <br>
•	Voluntary attrition dominates, indicating retention challenges <br>
•	Highest turnover among:<br>
	- Managers (~52%) <br>
	- Directors (~51%) <br>
  
**Attrition Drivers**

Top reasons employees leave:<br>
1.	Better job opportunities <br>
2.	Lack of flexible benefits <br>
3.	Salary dissatisfaction <br>

### 💡 Business Recommendations

**1. Improve Employee Retention**

•	Introduce competitive salary benchmarking <br>
•	Expand flexible work policies <br>
•	Strengthen career growth opportunities <br>

**2. Leadership Development**

•	Focus on manager and director retention <br>
•	Provide leadership training and performance support <br>

**3. Department-Specific Actions**

•	Address high turnover in R&D and Sales <br>
•	Conduct exit analysis to identify root causes <br>

**4. Diversity & Inclusion**

•	Promote diverse hiring strategies <br>
•	Improve gender and ethnicity representation <br>

**5. Workforce Strategy**

•	Optimize remote work model <br>
•	Expand hiring beyond major cities <br>
